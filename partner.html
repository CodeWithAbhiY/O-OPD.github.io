FAQ

Questions, answered

Is there any setup fee?

No. Listing your centre and getting set up on O-OPD has no setup fee.

How do patients book appointments?

Patients search by specialty, location, and date, pick an available OPD slot at your centre, and confirm online — 24/7.

Can I manage multiple doctors?

Yes. You can add multiple doctors, their departments, consultation fees, and individual availability schedules.

Does O-OPD support cancellations?

Yes. Patients can cancel within policy, slots are freed automatically, and applicable refunds are calculated transparently.

How are appointment confirmations handled?

Confirmations are automated — patients receive instant on-screen and email confirmation when a booking is made.
